Scattered rain expected over north, Central Luzon – PAGASA
Published January 31, 2018 6:53am

The Cordillera Administrative and Cagayan Valley Regions, as well as Aurora, would be cloudy with scattered rain due to the northeast monsoon, weather service PAGASA predicted in Wednesday morning forecast.

The Ilocos and Central Luzon Regions would also be under the influence of the northeast monsoon, and could thus expect partly cloudy to cloudy conditions with isolated rain.

The rest of the country would meanwhile be partly cloudy to cloudy with isolated rainshowers.

Winds would be strong to gale from the northeast over northern Luzon, where coastal waters would be rough to very rough.

Over the rest of Luzon and the eastern section of the Visayas, winds would be moderate to strong from the northeast to east, with moderate to rough seas.

Winds over the eastern section of Mindanao would be moderate to occasionally strong from the northeast to north with moderate to occasionally rough coastal waters.

Finally, winds over the rest of the country would be light to moderate from the northeast to east with slight to moderate coastal waters. — DVM, GMA News

Light to heavy rain expected over Mindanao, the Eastern Visayas
Published February 3, 2018 4:59am

Weather service PAGASA reported in a Saturday morning forecast that a low-pressure area (LPA) was 505 kilometers east of Hinatuan, Surigao del Sur at 3 a.m.

The LPA's trough was expected to bring cloudy conditions with scattered rainshowers and thunderstorms over Mindanao and the Eastern Visayas

The northeast monsoon was meanwhile expected to bring cloudy conditions with scattered rain over the Cagayan Valley Region, the Cordillera Administrative Region and Aurora; and partly cloudy to cloudy conditions with isolated rain over Metro Manila, the Ilocos Region, and the rest of Central Luzon.

The rest of the country would partly cloudy to cloudy with isolated rainshowers.

Winds would be strong to gale force from the northeast over northern and Central Luzon, where coastal waters would be rough to very rough.

Over the rest of Luzon and the eastern section of the Visayas, winds would be moderate to strong from the northeast to north with moderate to rough seas.

Winds over the rest of the country, winds would be light to moderate from the northeast to north with slight to moderate coastal waters. — DVM, GMA News

LPA spotted over southern Mindanao
Published February 1, 2018 6:07am 

The northeast monsoon will be affecting northern Luzon on Thursday while the trough of Low Pressure Area (LPA) will be affecting southern Mindanao, PAGASA reported in its early morning forecast.

Due to the LPA, Caraga, northern Mindanao, Zamboanga Peninsula, and Davao region will be cloudy with scattered to widespread rains and thunderstorms.

The coastal weather condition in extreme northern Luzon and the eastern section of the Visayas will be moderate to occasionally rough. — BAP, GMA News
