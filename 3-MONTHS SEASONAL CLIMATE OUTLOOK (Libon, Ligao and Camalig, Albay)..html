3-MONTHS SEASONAL CLIMATE OUTLOOK
(Libon, Ligao and Camalig, Albay).

Issued: January 31, 2017
Valid: January 31, 2017 - April 30, 2017
La Niña-like conditions continue, as impacts have been seen in some flood- and landslide-prone areas in the country.
Weather Forecast
Weather System
Weather System: Northeast monsoon, Tail end of cold front, Low Pressure Area (LPA), Tropical Cyclones, Ridge of High Pressure Area (HPA), Easterlies.
Fishing Advisory
Libon, Ligao and Camalig is expected to experience above normal rainfall for the month of February, near normal for the month of March and below normal rainfall for the month of April
Farming Advisory
Moderate to occasionally strong winds blowing from the northeast will prevail for the month of February. Light to moderate winds blowing from the east will prevail for the months of March and April.
Temperature
Average range of temperature is 23°C - 31°C
Relative Humidity
75% - 95%
Soil Moisture
Dry to moist soil moisture
Probability of Rain
February 0 or 1; March 0 or 1; April 0 or 1
